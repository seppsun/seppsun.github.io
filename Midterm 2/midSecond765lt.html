McLaren 765LT

Introduction

The McLaren 765LT is a track-focused, limited edition version of the 720S supercar. LT stands for "Longtail," which pays homage to the McLaren F1 GTR Longtail racing car. The 765LT features an extended rear, improved aerodynamics, reduced weight, and increased power.

The exterior of the 765LT is characterized by its aggressive design, featuring an elongated rear, front splitter, and large rear wing. The interior combines luxury and performance, with lightweight materials and a driver-focused cockpit.

Specs
  • Engine: 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8
  • Power: 755 horsepower
  • Torque: 590 lb-ft
  • Transmission: Seven-speed dual-clutch automatic
  • 0-60 mph: 2.7 seconds
  • Top speed: 205 mph
  • Weight: 2,952 pounds
  • Suspension: Proactive Chassis Control II
  • Brakes: Carbon-ceramic brakes
  • Tires: Pirelli P Zero Trofeo R tires

The McLaren 765LT is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, producing 755 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It can accelerate from 0-60 mph in just 2.7 seconds and reach a top speed of 205 mph. The car's Proactive Chassis Control II suspension and carbon-ceramic brakes provide exceptional handling and stopping power.
