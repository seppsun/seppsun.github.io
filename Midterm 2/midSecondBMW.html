BMW M4 CSL

Introduction

The BMW M4 CSL, also known as the "Coupe Sport Lightweight," is a high-performance version of the BMW M4 sports car. It is a limited edition model that was released in 2023, designed for car enthusiasts and track-day enthusiasts. The M4 CSL is the ultimate driving machine that promises pure driving pleasure, thanks to its lightweight construction, advanced aerodynamics, and powerful engine.

The exterior design of the BMW M4 CSL is sleek and aggressive, with a low profile and a wide stance. It features a carbon fiber roof, hood, and trunk lid, which reduces weight and improves the car's handling and stability. The car's front splitter, rear diffuser, and carbon fiber wing contribute to its aerodynamic performance. The interior of the M4 CSL is designed for performance and comfort, with lightweight bucket seats, carbon fiber trim, and advanced technology features.

Specs
  • Engine: 3.0-liter TwinPower Turbo inline-six engine
  • Power: 540 horsepower
  • Torque: 479 lb-ft
  • Transmission: Seven-speed dual-clutch automatic
  • 0-60 mph: 3.6 seconds
  • Top speed: 190 mph
  • Weight: 3,400 pounds
  • Suspension: Adaptive M suspension with electronically controlled dampers
  • Brakes: Lightweight high-performance carbon-ceramic brakes
  • Tires: Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 tires
Track Lap Times
