AMG GT R

Introduction

The AMG GT R is a high-performance sports car that combines power and luxury. The GT R is part of the AMG GT series, which is known for its aggressive styling and exceptional performance. The GT R promises an exhilarating driving experience, whether on the road or the track.

The exterior of the GT R is designed for maximum performance, with a long hood, aggressive front grille, and aerodynamic lines. The car also features an adjustable rear wing and active aerodynamics. The interior of the GT R is designed for comfort and luxury, with premium materials and advanced technology features.

Specs
  • Engine: 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8
  • Power: 577 horsepower
  • Torque: 516 lb-ft
  • Transmission: Seven-speed dual-clutch automatic
  • 0-60 mph: 3.5 seconds
  • Top speed: 198 mph
  • Weight:3,627 pounds
  • Suspension: AMG RIDE CONTROL sports suspension
  • Brakes: High-performance composite braking system
  • Tires: Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 tires

The AMG GT R is a true performance machine, with a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that delivers 577 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. The car can go from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.5 seconds and has a top speed of 198 mph. The GT R also features AMG RIDE CONTROL sports suspension, a high-performance composite braking system, and Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 tires for exceptional handling and control on the road or the track.
