Ferrari 296 GTB

Introduction

The Ferrari 296 GTB is a groundbreaking sports car from the iconic Italian automaker, introducing a hybrid powertrain to the mid-engine V6 lineup. The 296 GTB combines cutting-edge technology, stunning design, and exhilarating performance to deliver a unique driving experience that is both eco-friendly and powerful.

The exterior of the 296 GTB is characterized by its sleek lines and aerodynamic design. The interior focuses on driver engagement with premium materials and the latest in Ferrari's infotainment and driver assistance technologies.

Specs
  • Engine: 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 + electric motor
  • Power: 819 horsepower (combined)
  • Torque: 546 lb-ft
  • Transmission: Eight-speed dual-clutch automatic
  • 0-60 mph: 2.9 seconds
  • Top speed: 205 mph
  • Weight: 3,241 pounds
  • Suspension: Magnetorheological dampers
  • Brakes: Carbon-ceramic brakes
  • Tires:Michelin Pilot Sport 4S tires

The Ferrari 296 GTB boasts impressive performance figures, with a combined output of 819 horsepower, a 0-60 mph time of just 2.9 seconds, and a top speed of 205 mph. The car's magnetorheological dampers and carbon-ceramic brakes ensure exceptional handling and braking capabilities on the track and road alike.
