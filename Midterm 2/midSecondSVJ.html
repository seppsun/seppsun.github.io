Lamborghini Aventador SVJ

Introduction

The Lamborghini Aventador SVJ (SuperVeloce Jota) is a limited edition, track-focused version of the iconic Aventador supercar. The SVJ takes the performance capabilities of the Aventador to new heights with enhanced aerodynamics, lightweight construction, and a powerful V12 engine.

The exterior of the Aventador SVJ is characterized by its aggressive design, featuring a prominent rear wing and aero-optimized bodywork. The interior boasts a race-inspired cabin with lightweight materials and an emphasis on driver engagement.

Specs
  • Engine: 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12
  • Power: 759 horsepower
  • Torque: 531 lb-ft
  • Transmission: Seven-speed ISR single-clutch automated manual
  • 0-60 mph: 2.8 seconds
  • Top speed: 217 mph
  • Weight: 3,362 pounds
  • Suspension: Magneto-rheological dampers
  • Brakes: Carbon-ceramic brakes
  • Tires: Pirelli P Zero Trofeo R tires

The Lamborghini Aventador SVJ boasts a 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine that delivers 759 horsepower and 531 lb-ft of torque. The car can sprint from 0-60 mph in just 2.8 seconds and reach a top speed of 217 mph. Its magneto-rheological dampers and carbon-ceramic brakes contribute to the impressive handling and braking performance that the SVJ is known for.
