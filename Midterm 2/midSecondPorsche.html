Porsche GT3RS (992)

Introduction

The Porsche GT3RS (992) is the latest generation of Porsche's legendary GT3 line. It is designed to be a track-focused, high-performance sports car that offers an exceptional driving experience. The GT3RS is built with lightweight materials, advanced aerodynamics, and a powerful engine, making it one of the most exciting sports cars on the market.

The exterior of the GT3RS is designed for maximum aerodynamic performance, with a large rear wing and an aggressive front splitter. The car also features lightweight body panels and advanced suspension technology. The interior of the GT3RS is designed for performance and comfort, with racing-inspired seats and advanced technology features.

Specs
  • Engine: 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-six engine
  • Power: 502 horsepower
  • Torque: 346 lb-ft
  • Transmission: Seven-speed dual-clutch automatic
  • 0-60 mph: 3.2 seconds
  • Top speed: 198 mph
  • Weight: 3,241 pounds
  • Suspension: Porsche Active Suspension Management (PASM) with adaptive dampers
  • Brakes: Carbon-ceramic brakes
  • Tires: Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 tires

The Porsche GT3RS (992) is a high-performance sports car that delivers exceptional driving experience with its powerful 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-six engine producing 502 horsepower. The car can go from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.2 seconds and has a top speed of 198 mph. The GT3RS also features advanced suspension technology, carbon-ceramic brakes, and Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 tires, making it a true track-focused sports car.
