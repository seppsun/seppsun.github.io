Porsche 911 Turbo S (992)

Introduction

The Porsche 911 Turbo S (992) is a high-performance sports car that represents the pinnacle of Porsche's legendary 911 lineup. It combines exceptional performance with luxury and cutting-edge technology, making it an extraordinary vehicle for both road and track.

The exterior of the 911 Turbo S (992) is characterized by its iconic 911 silhouette, wide body, and aggressive aerodynamics. The interior is crafted with premium materials and features the latest in Porsche's infotainment and driver assistance technologies.

Specs
  • Engine: 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged flat-six
  • Power: 640 horsepower
  • Torque: 590 lb-ft
  • Transmission: Eight-speed PDK automatic
  • 0-60 mph: 2.6 seconds
  • Top speed: 205 mph
  • Weight: 3,636 pounds
  • Suspension: Porsche Active Suspension Management (PASM)
  • Brakes: Porsche Ceramic Composite Brakes (PCCB)
  • Tires: Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 tires

The Porsche 911 Turbo S (992) boasts impressive performance figures, with a 0-60 mph time of just 2.6 seconds and a top speed of 205 mph. The car's Porsche Active Suspension Management (PASM) and Porsche Ceramic Composite Brakes (PCCB) contribute to its exceptional handling and braking capabilities on the track.
